Kaaliye Consulting Group (KCG) is a management consultancy firm that provides
innovative and durable solutions to the Somali community,
and beyond through advisory and consulting services, reforming business
competitiveness, knowledge, and talent management.
Our projects have resulted in sustainable solutions in a variety of sectors,
including financial inclusion, economic growth,
good governance,
and resilience in the face of adversity. We engage experienced consultants
with decades of experience to ensure our clients’ success.
We support sustainable development and economic growth in the government,
private, and nonprofit sectors to help Somalia overcome challenges and prosper.
We define “impact & sustainability” differently depending on the unique challenges of each mission.
